The Problem

AI Demands Your Data

  • ChatGPT, Gemini, and every major AI log your prompts, train on your data, and retain conversations on their servers
  • 89% quarterly retention on ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) proves demand — but 67% of enterprises cite privacy as a top adoption barrier
  • DeepL processes your most sensitive documents in plaintext ($10–69/user/mo)
  • Summary tools like Otter.ai ($8–30/user/mo) ingest private meetings and conversations

Signal Users Have Zero Options

  • ~100M monthly active users chose Signal because they refuse to compromise on privacy
  • They're journalists protecting sources, activists in authoritarian regimes, lawyers with privileged communications, developers handling sensitive code
  • They want AI translation, summarization, and assistants — but won't use tools that can read their data
  • Today: no AI service exists that meets Signal-level privacy guarantees
Sources: Business of Apps, Backlinko — Signal statistics 2025–2026

The gap: A $11.4B chatbot market growing 29.6% CAGR — but zero products purpose-built for the 100M people who care most about privacy.

Why Signal Users Need Privacy More

Not "Normal" Users

  • Journalists & sources — a leaked translation query could identify a whistleblower or compromise a story
  • Activists & dissidents — in authoritarian regimes, an AI query about organizing could mean prison. Signal is #1 in Egypt, Iran, Saudi Arabia
  • Lawyers — attorney-client privilege requires AI tools cannot leak case data
  • Security researchers — querying AI about vulnerabilities on a logging platform creates an attack surface
  • Healthcare workers — HIPAA requires patient data in AI summaries is protected

No Other Platform Can Solve This

  • WhatsApp — owned by Meta. E2E encrypted but metadata harvested. Bot API sends data plaintext to bot servers
  • Telegram — NOT E2E encrypted by default. Server-side storage. Bot API = plaintext to developers
  • iMessage — E2E encrypted but Apple controls platform. No bot ecosystem, no TEE integration possible
  • Slack / Teams — enterprise admin access to all messages. No true user privacy
  • Only Signal — open source, independently audited, E2E by default, zero metadata, non-profit foundation

TEEs close the last gap. Signal encrypts the transport. TEEs encrypt the processing. Together: the first AI pipeline where privacy is mathematically guaranteed, not just promised in a ToS.
